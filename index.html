Getting started

  1. Pick a network above.
  2. Type a focus gene to center the view on its regulators and targets.
  3. Click any node to re-center, or an edge for interaction details.

Tip: the two validated networks (Pax3, TFAP2e) combine GRNboost2 predictions with morpholino knockdown and ChIP-seq — edges are colored by how they were confirmed.
